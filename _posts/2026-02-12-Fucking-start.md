# Fucking start!

I listened to some gamedev podcast (feeding a black whole) and read some hackernews post about getting a clean space to start working. There are positions on both sides. In my opinion, I like my desk clean, but often I think I want to clean to feel productive and follow my excuses to not do things that are a little more exhausting that the task my smart self wants to do.

So whats the issue. I like to clean my desk and get into routines and get into the gamedev headspace by scrolling through reddit gamedev and watching/listening to gamedev stuff. I need routines and the most important thing, longterm, is to be productive and do things despite the distractions and excuses. I dont know how to do that.

Having a clean start is a good beginning of the day i think. So making a plan in the evening. Making small progress and wins through routine and iron will in the morning. Starting up the pc, not getting distracted by browser tabs from weeks ago. Lets make a plan, and try to focuse on stuff.
