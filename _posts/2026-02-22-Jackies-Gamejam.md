# Jackies Gamejam

## Theme and prototype

I already started a day late even though I voted for the theme lol. Theme is "Signal".

- Electrical Signal, im thinking of a 3d world where satellites and satellite stations are used

Visual inspiration would be "Voices of the void". Even though I do NOT like horror games, this is the theme and the design I want to go for. 3D, controlling Ground Satellite Stations.

Mechanics:
- Limit movement of the satellite to x amounts, before one has to go out and recharge motors or until something gets broken
- Timewait mechanic, as the sky changes, orient yourself on real events
- typical sysadmin stuff, move files from a to b, restart servers, fix Servers, bring them back to sync etcetc

Regarding the narrative, I listened to "House featuring John Cale" and thought about a narrative experience. As the song is very cryptic and displays some melancholy, I thought about the player having other peoples video messages they receive. And with every message the partner gets more desperate as the world around him gets destroyed or something. And in the end, the origin of the message is yourself, you send these messages. To yourself, from the future. Could be combined with the world getting destroyed, or you sending yourself a message from the future telling you something is coming.

Ambience: What about a ground control station in Greenland? Implement snowstorms! Setting could be during the cold war when stations would be used to track soviet objects

Use DEFCON
Every Defcon level changes the color in the room

Random events that distract the player from being attentive to the radar
- Satellite gets stuck, manual fix needed (maybe among us style?)
- Power overload, need to reboot systems (which takes time, bootup pc takes time, everything goes dark, flashlight mechanic)
- snow on the satellite, bad signal (less reach, random blibs)
- maybe something heat related as its in snowy antarctica? Refueling stuff?
