# Consistent unit scale

As described in 2026-02-26 Prototyping, during prototyping the biggest issue was me not being able to judge if walls are to high or doors too narrow. There are multiple ways to circumvent that. 

- Establish a Human Scale Reference First (I did that intuitively) 
  ![](/assets/blog/2026-02-27-Consistent-unit-scale/Pasted image 20260227114329.png)
  - Player should be 1.80 meters
  - Doorways 2 meters
  - Room Ceilings 2.5 - 3 meters
  - a normal Desk 0.75 meters
